A hospital is implementing a program to improve service quality and productivity.
As part of this program the hospital management is attempting to measure and
evaluate patient satisfaction. Data is collected from 95 recently discharged patients. The variables are: 
Y: satisfaction, a subjective response measure on an increasing scale. 

The potential regressor variables are 
age: patient age, 
severity: an index measuring the severity of the patient’ s illness, 
surgical: an indicator of whether the patient is a surgical or medical patient (0 = surgical, 1 = medical),
anxiety level: an index measuring the patient ’ s anxiety level, 
day: the number of day the patient stay in hospital 
gender: the gender of patients (0=female, 1=male)
